Common Foot Problems

Dr. Langford treats foot and ankle problems for people of all ages, from infants to the elderly. Anyone experiencing warning signs such as pain, redness, swelling, cramping, numbness, itching, burning or abnormal shoe wear should be examined by a podiatrist. Chronic conditions that once meant years of pain, inconvenience and expense, can now be corrected or controlled.

For more information on these common foot problems please refer to the Family Foot Health Specialists' Resource Page
