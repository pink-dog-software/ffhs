Office Policies

Payment

Office visit co-payments and deductibles are payable at the time the service is rendered unless prior arrangements are made with the office manager. We accept cash, personal checks, Master Card and Visa. There is a $25.00 service fee for all returned personal checks.

HMO's and PPO's

This office is a participating provider for most insurance plans operating in West Tennessee, Arkansas and Mississippi. We are providers for Blue Cross, Aetna, Cigna, United Healthcare, Medicare, Humana, TriCare and many others. If you are not sure if our office is on your plan, please feel free to contact us and we will be happy tos verify this for you.

Please be advised, it is your responsibility to obtain all referrals required by your insurance plan.

Insurance Claims and Third Party Carriers

Health claims will be filed for you by our highly qualified staff. All possible efforts will be made to secure all benefits due you through your insurance carrier; however, you are responsible for any balance on your account.

Disability Claims

Our staff will be happy to file employment disability claims, as well as credit disability claims for you, however, there is a $10.00 fee for this service.
